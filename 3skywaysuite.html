About

Story:

Styra is an anonymous being that was sent out of a white hole knowing nothing of her powers. She was asleep for around ten billion years before arriving on a random planet known as "Perenorbius" in a region called "Fantastele Forest" by its inhabitants. There, she first awoke from her sleep and after staying there peacefully for around 5 billion years, she confronts a space pilot who would help begin her journey across worlds to figure out the reasoning for her existence. In the process, she will learn about the powers she holds to help her get closer to the mysterious answer. There are ten eternal lights known as the Omnilumens. They are guarded by seven celestial guardians each able to use their designated eternal lights, with a king guardian ruling over the eighth Omnilumen, an overlord guardian ruling over the ninth Omnilumen, and the master of the omniverse ruling the tenth Omnilumen (along with all ten).

  1. Amethion: Keeper Of Time
  2. Topazion: Keeper Of Energy
  3. Sapphirion: Keeper Of Space
  4. Citrinion: Keeper Of Sound
  5. Emeraldion: Keeper Of Life
  6. Diamondion: Keeper Of Reality
  7. Spessarion: Keeper Of Surreality
  8. Rubion (King Strage): King Of Death
  9. Onyxion (Overlord Creperus): Overlord Of Darkness
  10. Prismion (Master Prism): Master Of The Omniverse

Skyway Suite: Largest luxurious airship cruise known to galaxies far away as "The Skyway Suite" in the skies of the planet, Aviatopolis. Aviatopolis also has sky homes and smaller airships, while the ground below is filled with machines generating power for the planet. The protector of these skies belong to Sapphirion. It is here where Styra learns to use flight.

How To Play:

All moves from the previous level can be applied here, except now you can also fly! The score is now a 4X multiplier for every hit made against the enemy by flying up against them and not falling or, sliding into the enemy. You must reach a power level of 600 to finish the level while avoiding or, beating enemy attacks.

Settings:

You can play a music track on the home page by pressing "1" for the game music, "2" for the main theme soundtrack, "3" for the series soundtrack and it will automatically loop until you pause it by pressing "P".
