About

Story:

Styra is an anonymous being that was sent out of a bright light knowing nothing of her powers. She was asleep for around ten billion years before arriving on a random planet known as "Perenorbius" in a region called "Fantastele Forest" by its inhabitants. There, she first awoke from her sleep and after staying there peacefully for around five billion years, she confronts a space pilot who would help begin her journey across worlds to figure out the reasoning for her existence. In the process, she will learn about the powers she holds to help her get closer to the mysterious answer. There are ten eternal lights known as the Omnilumens. They are guarded by seven celestial guardians each able to use their designated eternal lights, with a king guardian ruling over the eighth Omnilumen, an overlord guardian ruling over the ninth Omnilumen, and the master of the omniverse ruling the tenth Omnilumen (along with all ten).

  1. Amethion: Keeper Of Time
  2. Topazion: Keeper Of Energy
  3. Sapphirion: Keeper Of Space
  4. Citrinion: Keeper Of Sound
  5. Emeraldion: Keeper Of Life
  6. Diamondion: Keeper Of Reality
  7. Spessarion: Keeper Of Surreality
  8. Rubion (King Strage): King Of Death
  9. Onyxion (Overlord Creperus): Overlord Of Darkness
  10. Prismion (Master Prism): Master Of The Omniverse

Whimsical Woods: The birthplace of life on the planet Prima which is the first world to have life. In its entirety, this world has giant rainforest biomes. The fifth Omnilumen powers the planet at the center of its birthplace. Emeraldion gave infinite growth to this planet which means nothing in Prima can die. Styra must use the powers she already has to help her fight.

How To Play:

All moves from the previous level can be applied here. You must reach a power level of 1,000 to finish the level while avoiding or, beating enemy attacks.

Settings:

You can play a music track on the home page by pressing "1" for the game music, "2" for the main theme soundtrack, "3" for the series soundtrack and it will automatically loop until you pause it by pressing "P".
